1) Vocabulary & Clarifications

Roscón de Reyes
King's Cake – A round sweet bread eaten for Epiphany.
Nata
Cream – Usually whipped cream in cakes.
Pantalla
Screen – Computer or device screen.
Caber
To fit – Describes capacity.
Valer
To cost / be worth.
Sin duda
Without a doubt.
Inolvidable
Unforgettable.

2) Periphrastic Future (ir + a)

Yo voy a cantar.
I am going to sing.
Vamos a ir a la iglesia.
We are going to go to church.

3) Simple Future (Futuro Simple)

Same endings for all verbs: é, ás, á, emos, éis, án
Tener → Tendr-
tendré, tendrás, tendrá, tendremos, tendréis, tendrán

4) Practice Answers

El próximo año, tendré más dinero.
Next year, I will have more money.
Yo voy a aprender español.
I am going to learn Spanish.
